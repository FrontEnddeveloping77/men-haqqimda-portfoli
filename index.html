Men haqqimda ma'lumot!

O'zbekcha

Mening ismim Abdulaziz. Familyam Avazov. Men 2012-yil 25-avgustda O’zbekiston Respublikasi Namangan Shaxrida tug’ilganman. Xozirgacha SPC, Ziyokor va Investta’lim o’quv markazlarida matematika va ingliz tili var us tillariga o’qiganman. Ingliz tilini ham matematika fanini ham birinchi bo’lib SPC o’quv markazida 2024-yil yozda boshlaganman. Ingliz tilini president maktabiga kirish va ingliz tilida xoxlagan joyimda gaplasha olishim uchun o’qishni boshlaganman. Matematikani ham president maktabiga kirish va hisob-kitob qilayotganda qiynalmaslik uchun o’qiganman. 2023-yilning 15-avgustida Prezident maktabiga kirish uchun birinchi imtixon bo’lib o’tdi, imtixon natijalari 10 kun ichida chiqdi, imtixon natijalarini menga matematika ustozim ko’rib berdi, birinchi imtixondan 478-o’rinda o’tgan ekanman. Ikkinchi imtixon esa 2023-yil 30-avgustda bo’lib o’tdi, uning natijasi ham 10 kun ichida chiqdi, ikkinchi imtixon natijasini ham menga matematika ustozim ko’rib berdi, men ikkinchi imtixondan 411-o’rinni olib yiqilgan ekanman. Shundan so’ng 2023-yil 4-sentyabrdan 45-sonli maktabda rus sinfda o’qishni davom etdim. Sentyabr oyida dadamga qo’ng’iroq qilishdi ular: sizning o’g’lingiz ixtisoslashtirilgan maktabga qabul qilindi deyishdi, lekin men o’qishni istamadim chunki masofa uzog’ edi, hamda menga kursga borib til o’rganish qulayroq ko’rindi. 2023-yil sentyabr oyidan Ziyokor o’quv markazida ingliz tiliga o’qishni boshladim, u yerda umuman imtixonlardan yiqilmagan xolda 11 oy o’qidim, Ziyokorda men ko’p do’stlar ortirdim hamda ingliz tilini o’rgandim. 2024-yil sentyabr oyidan yangi o’quv yiliga men boshqa Namangan Shahar 1-sonli maktabga ko’chib o’tdim. U yerda darslar tushlikgacha bo’lar ekan. Men Ziyokor o’quv markazidagilarga aytdim ular menga ertalabga o’z darajamda guruh yo’q deyishdi. Men ingliz tilida o’qishni toxtatdim va 4 oy investta’limda rus tiliga bordim, u yerda ham yangi do’stlar ortirdim va rus tilini o’rgandim. 2025-yil yanvardan DataSite o’quv markazida FrontEnd dasturchi bo’lish uchun o’qishni boshladim. Xozirda men HTML va CSS dasturlash tillarini tugatib, JavaScript dasturlash tilini o’rganyapman. JavaScript dasturlash tilini ham kursda ham youtubdagi onlayn darsliklar orqali o’rganyapman chunki shu yozning iyun oyida imtixon bo’ladi, agar men imtixondan o’tsam meni Toshkent Shahriga amaliyot uchun olib ketishadi. Mening xozirdagi maqsadim FrontEnd dasturlash tilini to’liq tugatib ingliz va rus tillarini boshlash chunki menga ingilz va rus tili juda muhim. Buning sababi dasturlashda ko’p mijozlar asosan chet davlatlaridan bo’ladi, hamda bu tillar men chet davlatlariga ish yuzasidan yoki boshqa ish bilan chiqganimda yordam beradi.

Inlizcha

My name is Abdulaziz. My surname is Avazov. I was born on August 25, 2012, in the city of Namangan, Republic of Uzbekistan. Up to now, I have studied mathematics, English, and Russian at SPC, Ziyokor, and InvestTa’lim educational centers. I started learning both English and mathematics for the first time at the SPC center in the summer of 2024. I began learning English to be able to enter the Presidential School and to be able to speak English wherever I want. I studied mathematics as well to enter the Presidential School and to avoid difficulties in calculations. On August 15, 2023, the first entrance exam for the Presidential School was held. The results were announced within 10 days, and my math teacher checked them for me — I had passed the first exam ranking 478th. The second exam was held on August 30, 2023. Its results also came out within 10 days. My math teacher checked them again, and I ranked 411th in the second exam, which meant I didn’t pass. After that, starting from September 4, 2023, I continued my education in the Russian class of School No. 45. In September, my father received a phone call informing him that I had been accepted into a specialized school. However, I chose not to attend because the school was far away, and it seemed more convenient for me to attend a course and learn languages instead. Starting from September 2023, I began studying English at the Ziyokor education center. I studied there for 11 months without failing any exams. I made many friends there and improved my English. In September 2024, I transferred to Namangan City School No. 1 for the new academic year. Classes at this school are held in the mornings. I informed the staff at Ziyokor, and they told me there were no groups available at my level in the mornings. So, I stopped studying English and attended Russian language classes for 4 months at InvestTa’lim. There, I also made new friends and learned Russian. Since January 2025, I have been studying at the DataSite education center to become a Front-End developer. Currently, I have completed HTML and CSS and am learning JavaScript. I’m studying JavaScript both at the course and through online tutorials on YouTube, because there will be an exam this June. If I pass the exam, I will be taken to Tashkent for an internship. My current goal is to fully complete Front-End development and start learning English and Russian again, because these languages are very important to me. The reason is that most clients in programming are from foreign countries, and these languages will help me when I go abroad for work or other purposes.
