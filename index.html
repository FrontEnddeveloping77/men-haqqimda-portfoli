Men haqqimda ma'lumot!

O'zbekcha

Mening ismim Abdulaziz. Familyam Avazov. Men 2012-yil 25-avgustda O’zbekiston Respublikasi Namangan Shaxrida tug’ilganman. Xozirgacha SPC, Ziyokor va Investta’lim o’quv markazlarida matematika va ingliz tili va rus tillariga o’qiganman. Ingliz tilini ham matematika fanini ham birinchi bo’lib SPC o’quv markazida 2023-yil yozda boshlaganman. Ingliz tilini president maktabiga kirish va ingliz tilida xoxlagan joyimda gaplasha olishim uchun o’qishni boshlaganman. Matematikani ham president maktabiga kirish va hisob-kitob qilayotganda qiynalmaslik uchun o’qiganman. 2023-yilning 15-avgustida Prezident maktabiga kirish uchun birinchi imtixon bo’lib o’tdi, imtixon natijalari 10 kun ichida chiqdi, imtixon natijalarini menga matematika ustozim ko’rib berdi, birinchi imtixondan 478-o’rinda o’tgan ekanman. Ikkinchi imtixon esa 2023-yil 30-avgustda bo’lib o’tdi, uning natijasi ham 10 kun ichida chiqdi, ikkinchi imtixon natijasini ham menga matematika ustozim ko’rib berdi, men ikkinchi imtixondan 411-o’rinni olib yiqilgan ekanman. Shundan so’ng 2023-yil 4-sentyabrdan 45-sonli maktabda rus sinfda o’qishni davom etdim. Sentyabr oyida dadamga qo’ng’iroq qilishdi ular: sizning o’g’lingiz ixtisoslashtirilgan maktabga qabul qilindi deyishdi, lekin men o’qishni istamadim chunki masofa uzog’ edi, hamda menga kursga borib til o’rganish qulayroq ko’rindi. 2023-yil sentyabr oyidan Ziyokor o’quv markazida ingliz tiliga o’qishni boshladim, u yerda umuman imtixonlardan yiqilmagan xolda 11 oy o’qidim, Ziyokorda men ko’p do’stlar ortirdim hamda ingliz tilini o’rgandim. 2024-yil sentyabr oyidan yangi o’quv yiliga men boshqa Namangan Shahar 1-sonli maktabga ko’chib o’tdim. U yerda darslar tushlikgacha bo’lar ekan. Men Ziyokor o’quv markazidagilarga aytdim ular menga ertalabga o’z darajamda guruh yo’q deyishdi. Men ingliz tilida o’qishni toxtatdim va 4 oy investta’limda rus tiliga bordim, u yerda ham yangi do’stlar ortirdim va rus tilini o’rgandim. 1-sonli maktabdan 2025-yil fevral oyida chiqib ketdim chunki u yerdagi sinfimda bolalar haddan tashqari ko'p edi va darslar biroz tushinib bo'lmas, o'qituvchilar ham bolalar soni ko'pligidan hammaga birdek vaqt ajrata olishmas edi. 2025-yil yanvardan DataSite o’quv markazida FrontEnd dasturchi bo’lish uchun o’qishni boshladim. Xozirda men HTML va CSS dasturlash tillarini tugatib, JavaScript dasturlash tilini o’rganyapman. JavaScript dasturlash tilini ham kursda ham youtubdagi onlayn darsliklar orqali o’rganyapman chunki shu yozning iyun oyida imtixon bo’ladi, agar men imtixondan o’tsam meni Toshkent Shahriga amaliyot uchun olib ketishadi. Mening xozirdagi maqsadim FrontEnd dasturlash tilini to’liq tugatib ingliz va rus tillarini boshlash chunki menga ingilz va rus tili juda muhim. Buning sababi dasturlashda ko’p mijozlar asosan chet davlatlaridan bo’ladi, hamda bu tillar men chet davlatlariga ish yuzasidan yoki boshqa ish bilan chiqganimda yordam beradi.

The second exam was held on August 30, 2023, and its results were also announced within 10 days. My math teacher checked them again, and I found out I had ranked 411th in the second exam and unfortunately did not pass. After that, starting from September 4, 2023, I continued my studies in the Russian-language class at School No. 45. In September, my father received a call saying that I had been accepted into a specialized school, but I chose not to attend because the distance was too far, and attending a language course seemed more convenient for me. Starting from September 2023, I began studying English at the Ziyokor education center. I studied there for 11 months without failing any exams. At Ziyokor, I made many friends and improved my English. From September 2024, I transferred to another school — Namangan City School No. 1 — for the new academic year. There, classes were held only until noon. I informed the staff at Ziyokor, but they told me that there were no groups at my level available in the morning. Because of that, I stopped studying English and attended Russian classes at InvestTa’lim for 4 months. There, I also made new friends and learned Russian. I left School No. 1 in February 2025 because the class size was too large, and it was difficult to understand the lessons. The teachers also couldn't give enough attention to each student due to the high number of students. Since January 2025, I have been studying at the DataSite education center to become a Front-End developer. So far, I have completed learning HTML and CSS, and now I’m learning JavaScript. I study JavaScript both at the course and through online tutorials on YouTube, because there will be an exam this June. If I pass, I will be taken to Tashkent for an internship. My current goal is to fully complete Front-End development and then restart learning English and Russian, because these two languages are very important to me. The reason is that in programming, most clients are from foreign countries, and these languages will help me when I travel abroad for work or any other purpose.
